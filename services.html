Services

Cutting-edge solutions to transform biological data into impactful discoveries.

Our comprehensive range of services covers every stage of the research and development lifecycle, from raw data analysis to scalable pipeline implementation.

Bioinformatics Analysis

Our bioinformatics expertise spans transcriptomics, metagenomics, epigenomics, and multi-omics integration. We employ state-of-the-art statistical models, machine learning algorithms, and visualization tools to deliver insights that drive discovery. Our analysts ensure data quality control (QC), normalization, differential expression analysis, and results interpretation tailored to your research questions.

Biotechnology & R&D

We accelerate R&D workflows through custom assay design, gene editing, molecular cloning, and protein engineering. Our biotechnology team works closely with you to develop scalable protocols that ensure reproducible results, enabling faster prototyping and product development in therapeutics, diagnostics, and beyond.

Genomic & Proteomic Services

From next-generation sequencing (NGS) data processing to variant calling, from proteomics quantification to microbiome profiling, we provide end-to-end solutions. Our methods ensure accurate gene expression profiling, SNP/INDEL detection, functional annotation, and pathway analysis, giving you a complete molecular portrait of your biological system.

Custom Data Pipelines

We build tailored data pipelines to automate analysis workflows, integrate LIMS systems, and leverage cloud computing for scalability. Streamline your data handling, improve turnaround times, and enhance reproducibility with our custom pipeline solutions designed for long-term sustainability and growth.
